About Me

Welcome to my gallery! I'm passionate about creating Abstract looping animations & digital art using Blender. My journey into 3D started as a creative exploration and over time, it became an exciting hobby where I experiment with movement, lighting, and procedural techniques. I enjoy pushing the limits of Blender's animation tools to craft visually engaging loops, often inspired by patterns, physics and surreal features. This gallery showcases some of my favorite works, where each one showcases my skill & imagination.

Beyond animation, I continuously explore new techniques in blender, refining my workflow and expanding my artistic approach. Whether it's procedural animations, shader experiments or motion loops, I love the challenge of bringing ideas to life in a visually captivating way.

About Blender

Blender is a powerful open-source 3D creation software widely used for animation, modeling, rendering, VFX and game development. Known for its versatility, Blender offers an extensive toolset, including a node-based shader editor, sculpting features, physics simulations and a built-in compositor. Its real-time rendering engine, Eevee, and ray-tracing renderer, Cycles, allow artists to create stunning visuals and impressive realism.

What sets Blender apart is its community-driven development & accessibility --it's free to use and constantly evolving with contributions from developers and artists worldwide. Whether you're beginner or an experienced creator, Blender provides endless possibilities for artistic expression, making it a favorite tool among digital artists, animators and hobbyists like me.
